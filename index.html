Meet-Up Android App

This Android Application allows students at UBC the ability to find other students to meet up with in between mutual breaks in their schedules. This app displays the students' schedules on a map of UBC and shows the route between their classes while highlighting dining locations on campus that they can meet up at.

This application was developed as part of my Software Construction class at UBC (CPSC 210). We were given the UI and some supporting methods but I implemented the client side.
